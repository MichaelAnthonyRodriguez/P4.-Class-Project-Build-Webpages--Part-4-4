QuickSearch

QuickSearch is a simple chat-style page where users can describe what they want to watch in their own words. This version is a static mockup, but it shows what the experience could look like.

QuickSearch Chat
QuickSearch
Hello! What kind of movie are you feeling like watching?
You
I want something funny and light with friends hanging out.
QuickSearch
Great choice. You might enjoy a college comedy or a slice-of-life story about roommates. Look for:
  • Short episodes that are easy to binge.
  • Comedy or “feel-good” tags.
  • Shows centered around a friend group.

This demo does not send real messages. It only shows what the interface might look like.

  • Users type in a natural sentence instead of choosing from long menus.
  • The system could match mood, genre, and tone to a list of titles.
  • Results could be connected to the Watchlist so users can save picks.
